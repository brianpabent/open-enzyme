Homotetramer. Each subunit ≈ 34 kDa (301–302 residues), giving a native molecular mass of ~136 kDa.  
*Primary source*: X‑ray crystallography of recombinant *A. flavus* uricase shows a tetramer (dimer of dimers) [Colloc’h et al. (1997) *Nat. Struct. Biol.* **4**, 947–952].  
*Evidence level*: in vitro.

*Evidence level*: in vitro / structural.
