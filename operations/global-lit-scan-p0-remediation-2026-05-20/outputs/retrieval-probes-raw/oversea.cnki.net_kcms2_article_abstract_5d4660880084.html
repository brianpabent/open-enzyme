Screening,separation and mass spectrometry analysis of xanthine oxidase inhibitors in Phellinus igniarius

Chinese Full Text Attached video

LIU Zhen HOU Wanchao NIU Huazhou YUN Haocheng LIU Chunming LI Sainan ZHANG Yuchi Central Laboratory,Changchun Normal University

长春师范大学中心实验室

Abstract： A new method for the rapid screening and separation of xanthine oxidase inhibitors from Phellinus igniarius was developed using a combination of mathematical modeling,chromatography and mass spectrometry. The extraction process of flavonoid active ingredients in Phellinus igniarius was optimized by using mathematical modeling combined with response surface method,the ultrafiltration-mass spectrometry technique was applied to rapidly screen the inhibitors of xanthine oxidase in Phellinus igniarius;Under the guidance of activity,the active ingredients in Phellinus igniarius were separated and purified by high speed countercurrent chromatography(HSCCC),high performance liquid chromatography-high resolution mass spectrometry coupled with nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy was used for the structural identification of the active ingredients in Phellinus igniarius. The results showed that the extraction rate of Phellinus igniarius flavonoids was 24.95 mg/g,which was better than the response surface test;three xanthine oxidase inhibitors with the purity of above 90% were screened and isolated from Phellinus igniarius,and their inhibition effect was in the order of phelligridin D>phelligridin E>Fibrocidin A. This method is rapid,sensitive,and can provide support for the study of the pharmacological action of Phellinus igniarius,and also provide feasible method and theoretical support for the development of Phellinus igniarius food.
