What is CNKI AI？

CNKI AI is an AI academic research tool developed by CNKI exclusively for global users. Leveraging large model technology, CNKI’s 380 million cross-discipline literature resources, and achievements of over 40 million Chinese scholars, it delivers precise literature mining, trustworthy knowledge comprehension, and comprehensive writing support.

Using technologies like multilingual solutions and intent recognition, it rapidly locates high-value literature and generates credible content to help you efficiently grasp core viewpoints. It also supports automatic generation of high-quality abstracts, assists academic translation and writing, and offers tools like originality detection, citation generation, and intelligent typesetting – building a complete AI research ecosystem from literature discovery to output.
