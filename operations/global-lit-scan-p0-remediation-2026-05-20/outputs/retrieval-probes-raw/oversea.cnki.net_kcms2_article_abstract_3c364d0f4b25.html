Study on the Cordyceps militaris for lowering uric acid of patients with hyperuricemia

Chinese Full Text Attached video

YIN Yan-yan WU Ping-ping ZHU Bao-jun GONG Cheng HUANG Ru-qiang College of Life Science,South China Normal University South China Normal University Hospital

华南师范大学生命科学学院 华南师范大学医院

Abstract： Objective This is a study of Cordyceps militaris powder on reducing effect of uric acid in patients with hyperuricemia. Methods 22 case of patients with hyperuricemia which have no serious cardiovascular,liver,kidney,lung and other vital organs disease were selected to take the cordyceps militaris powder. After taking a period of time,the levels of serum uric acid( UA),blood urea nitrogen( BUN),creatinine( CREA) were measured. Results One month later,there were significant differences in all indicators( P < 0. 05) : efficiency rate decreasing UA reached to 77. 3%; BUN reached to 61. 9%; CREA reached to85. 7%. Conclusion The results showed that Cordyceps militaris powder can significantly reduce the levels of UA,BUN,CREA and has a good effect on reducing uric acid in patients with hyperuricemia. More Reset
