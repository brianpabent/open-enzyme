    Strategic Reflections Queue¶

    Content-triggered (not calendar-triggered) reflections that need to fire when accumulated substance crosses a maturity threshold. Distinct from the action queue above — these are platform-level reframes, not unit-of-work tasks. The daemon surfaces them on each sweep so they don't get lost.

    Reflection Trigger Outcome
    Open Enzyme platform-framing reframe — does the broader matrix-tracks portfolio justify expanding the project framing from "engineered enzymes in koji" to "solve gout, every avenue, fully open"? Three scope-pages now exist as concrete instances: LBP chassis (peer-track strain-library; commercial-pharma sub-track), siRNA / URAT1 modality (discovery-engine output; non-microbial), and TCM × modern rigor (methodology lens; discovery-engine output with comp-013 triage now landed). Other matrix tracks (Q141K pharmacological chaperones, mRNA-IL-1RA pulse therapy, engineered exosomes, phages) remain as matrix-row scope only. After both peer-track Phase 2 queues accumulate substance — LBP track P2-1 through P2-6 (engineering + commercial + regulatory lit scans; comp-008; H02 full; chassis matrix) AND siRNA / URAT1 track P2-1 through P2-6 (kidney-tropic conjugate chemistry + commercial + regulatory lit scans; comp-009; H03 full; vs. pozdeutinurad comparative) AND TCM track P2-1, P2-3 through P2-6 (P2-2 closed by comp-013 2026-05-06; remaining are classical formula lit scans + Smilax deep-dive + Si Miao San decomposition + H04 full + bioavailability characterization). Reflection note pre-positioned in open-enzyme-vision.md §4. Decide whether to reframe open-enzyme-vision.md §4's leading sentence, the README, and platform-thesis pages. The project name itself (Open Enzyme) is a downstream decision after that reframe — naming follows substance. The accumulated peer-track substance is the trigger; the absence of that substance keeps the koji-first framing intact by default.
    Chaperone framework predictive-power validation — is the framework actually predictive, or is it sophisticated retrofit? The 2026-05-06 walkthrough surfaced two material weakening corrections (Wakai 2019's "supra-additive 3-cellulase synergy" was actually copy-number + promoter engineering, not synergy; Zhang 2006's "cross-class combinations 5× advantage" was cross-paper Yu 2017 comparison, intra-paper advantage is 1.2-1.5×). The framework's central strategic claim ("cross-class helper combinations are highest-leverage capacity-expansion lever") is materially weakened. The triple-cassette prediction (architecture-adjusted 0.35-0.65) is the first prospective test the framework has made — not retrofit. When the §1.9 Lf-alone arm + dual-cassette arm + (if pursued) triple-cassette arm wet-lab data lands. Lf-alone titer >500 mg/L would shift the upper bound of the triple-cassette prediction more favorably; titer <40 mg/L would confirm the lower bound. If the predicted triple synergy 0.45-0.55 lands accurately within ±0.10 of measured, that's first real validation that the load-class scoring rules have cross-substrate predictive power. If it lands >0.20 off, the framework needs revision — the qualitative prediction (PDI substrates compete) may stand, but the quantitative coefficients calibrated from non-koji data don't transfer. Decide whether to (a) keep using the framework as a prospective design tool, (b) restrict it to qualitative direction-only claims, © recalibrate coefficients against the new koji data, or (d) deprecate the framework in favor of pcSecKoji-style proteome-constrained model.
