    Synthesis — Action Queue¶

    How to read this file¶

    This is the action queue for the sweep daemon and manual synthesis passes. Entries appear when a sweep surfaces a new connection, contradiction, or experiment; entries leave when the work lands in the canonical wiki page (or is folded into validation-experiments.md / open-questions.md).

    The full audit trail of every sweep — including findings that have already landed — lives in logs/v4-synthesis-*.md and logs/v4-peer-review-*.md. Don't re-state finished work here; if you want the history, go to the log.
