# Open Enzyme platform-framing reframe

## Reflection

Does the broader matrix-tracks portfolio justify expanding the project framing from "engineered enzymes in koji" to "solve gout, every avenue, fully open"? Three scope-pages now exist as concrete instances:

- [LBP chassis](../../wiki/engineered-lbp-chassis.md) (peer-track strain-library; commercial-pharma sub-track)
- [siRNA / URAT1 modality](../../wiki/sirna-urat1-modality.md) (discovery-engine output; non-microbial)
- [TCM × modern rigor](../../wiki/tcm-modern-rigor-intersection.md) (methodology lens; discovery-engine output with comp-013 triage now landed)

Other matrix tracks (Q141K pharmacological chaperones, mRNA-IL-1RA pulse therapy, engineered exosomes, phages) remain as matrix-row scope only.

## Trigger

After both peer-track Phase 2 queues accumulate substance:

- **LBP track P2-1 through P2-6** — engineering + commercial + regulatory lit scans; comp-008; H02 full; chassis matrix
- **siRNA / URAT1 track P2-1 through P2-6** — kidney-tropic conjugate chemistry + commercial + regulatory lit scans; comp-009; H03 full; vs. pozdeutinurad comparative
- **TCM track P2-1, P2-3 through P2-6** — P2-2 closed by comp-013 2026-05-06; remaining are classical formula lit scans + Smilax deep-dive + Si Miao San decomposition + H04 full + bioavailability characterization

Reflection note pre-positioned in [`open-enzyme-vision.md` §4](../../wiki/etc/open-enzyme-vision.md).

## Outcome

Decide whether to reframe `open-enzyme-vision.md` §4's leading sentence, the README, and platform-thesis pages. The project name itself (Open Enzyme) is a downstream decision after that reframe — naming follows substance. The accumulated peer-track substance is the trigger; the absence of that substance keeps the koji-first framing intact by default.
