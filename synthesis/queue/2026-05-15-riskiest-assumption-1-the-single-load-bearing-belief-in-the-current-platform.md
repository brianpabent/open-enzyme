The single load-bearing belief in the current platform thesis that is least supported by the corpus is **the assumption that the gut-lumen uricase sink produces a clinically meaningful serum urate reduction in a typical (non-CKD) gout cohort, in the −0.5 to −1.0 mg/dL band predicted by the comp-019 flux model.** This belief is asserted in [`cross-validation.md` §Claim 1](./cross-validation.md) (feasibility rating 6.5/10), [`gut-lumen-sink.md`](./gut-lumen-sink.md) (the entire page), and [`open-enzyme-vision.md`](./open-enzyme-vision.md) §5 (the platform thesis), and is formalized as the H08 falsification card stub at [`hypotheses/H08-gut-lumen-sink-platform-thesis.md`](./hypotheses/H08-gut-lumen-sink-platform-thesis.md). The supporting evidence is: (a) ABCG2-mediated intestinal urate secretion accounts for ~33% of daily elimination (Miyazaki 2025, PMID 40033341 — first direct human in-vivo measurement), (b) the comp-019 Monte Carlo flux model (n=5,000) anchored to Miyazaki's measurements predicts genotype-stratified ΔSUA in the −0.5 to −1.0 mg/dL range, and (c) ALLN-346 Phase 2a Study 201 showed a signal in CKD patients. The evidence *against* this belief is: (a) ALLN-346 Phase 2a Study 202 (the broader, more typical-gout cohort) showed 0–5% SUA reduction with no significance vs. placebo, and the program terminated with 19/200 patients enrolled; (b) **zero** uricase trials (oral or systemic) have ever stratified results by ABCG2 Q141K genotype — the Q141K × allopurinol response literature is rich, the Q141K × uricase response literature is empty; (c) the comp-019 flux model is prospective and entirely unvalidated against any human cohort; (d) renal compensation for increased gut urate elimination is unmeasured — the model assumes ≤30% offset, but the true offset could be higher and could vary by CKD stage. The gap between the internally consistent in-silico model and the absent clinical validation is the widest in the platform. If the real-world typical-gout effect is <0.5 mg/dL, the platform's urate-lowering value proposition collapses from "core mechanism" to "mild adjunct," reshaping the commercial framing, the self-experiment framing, and the priority of downstream peer tracks. This assumption was identified as the #1 riskiest assumption in the 2026-05-09 sweep and committed as H08 on 2026-05-15; the killshot menu (ALLN-346 Study 202 genotype-stratified re-analysis attempt, n=1 self-experiment, Phase 2b RCT) remains queued. The assumption has not weakened since H08 was committed — but it has also not gained any new supporting evidence from the current sweep's trigger files.
