A fructose challenge on an XO-inhibited background tests *residual XO-independent urate production* and *uricase activity*, but does NOT test whether exertion/fatigue triggers flares via the mechanical-trigger axis. This discriminates the dominant uncertainty in the mechanical-triggers page with zero additional budget beyond what the self-experiment protocol already allocates. The genotype-informed-supplement-workflow's step 5 (biomarker tracking) already requires Brian to log flares with temporal granularity — the exertion challenge simply adds a **structured exposure** to the logging protocol.
