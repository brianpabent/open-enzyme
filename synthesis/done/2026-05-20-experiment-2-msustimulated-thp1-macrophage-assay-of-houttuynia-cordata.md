## ✓ Actioned 2026-05-21 (landed as validation-experiments.md §1.30)

**Three-arm Houttuynia fraction comparison landed at [`validation-experiments.md` §1.30](../../wiki/validation-experiments.md)** this walk.

Protocol:
- **Arm A:** HCPM (19.1 kDa Fudan fraction, Lu 2018 PMC5925397 protocol; gold-standard purified preparation; sourcing via Chen Daofeng / Fudan collaboration request or independent ethanol-precipitation + DEAE-Sepharose ion-exchange + Sephadex G-100 size-exclusion preparation)
- **Arm B:** Crude HCP (boiled-water extract of *Houttuynia cordata* whole-herb — home-preparation reference, parallels mushroom β-glucan crude vs. purified comparison)
- **Arm C:** Commercial Houttuynia capsule extract (three brands; consumer-product representative)

Each arm: dose-response (1, 10, 100, 1000 μg/mL) in MSU-stimulated THP-1 macrophages (1 × 10⁶/well, LPS pre-prime 100 ng/mL × 3 hr, MSU challenge 100 μg/mL × 6 hr). Vehicle + nigericin positive controls.

Readouts: IL-1β supernatant ELISA (primary, CP3/CP4 NLRP3 axis), IL-6 (secondary), C3a + sC5b-9 (CP0 complement-axis), CCK-8 cell viability (cytotoxicity confound check).

Decision rules cover four outcomes — HCPM works + commercial fails (promote with consumer-product caveat), all three equivalent (promote to dietary stack), none work (deprioritize Houttuynia), crude HCP ≥ HCPM (purification not load-bearing).

Cost ~$2,000–3,000 CRO assay, 4–6 weeks. Operational bottleneck is HCPM sourcing (Chen Daofeng / Fudan collaboration vs. in-house Lu 2018 protocol preparation).

This is the wet-lab gate that comp-039 §3.3 noted as the cheapest discriminating test for HCP, complementary to comp-040 (the CFH-depleted serum MSU-crystal definitive falsification test for the CFH-independence classification).
