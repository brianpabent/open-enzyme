## Verdict: GREEN

**URAT1 mRNA has accessible siRNA target sites; the H03 mechanistic killshot does not fire.** Of 1488 21-nt windows scanned across the SLC22A12 CDS, 10 candidates survive the combined Reynolds + Ui-Tei + immunogenicity + cross-mammalian conservation pipeline with composite scores 66–81/100. Top candidate (CDS 1561, AA 521 `LPLPDTI`) carries an antisense guide `5'-UAUAGUAUCUGGCAAAGGUAG-3'` at 38% GC, Reynolds 6/8, 100% AA conservation across human/chimp/mouse/rat — same guide should work in rodent preclinical PK/PD AND a human therapeutic.

## Limitations

**CDS-only, back-translation surrogate.** NCBI was not in the analysis-environment allowed-host list; UTRs were not analysed and the CDS was generated by seeded weighted sampling from Kazusa human codon frequencies (GC 56.5%, matching natural human-CDS character) rather than the actual NM_144585.3 mRNA. **The AA-window column and conservation columns are robust; the exact nucleotide-sequence column requires re-derivation from real RefSeq mRNA before oligo synthesis.** Additional caveats — ViennaRNA RNAplfold accessibility approximated with a Tinoco-style stack-energy surrogate (r ~ 0.6–0.7 with RNAplfold per Tafer 2008); no BLAST against full human transcriptome for seed off-target burden; conservation at AA level not nucleotide level — are all wet-lab handoff items, fully documented in [`experiments/comp-009-urat1-sirna-target-site-selection/README.md`](../comp-009-urat1-sirna-target-site-selection/README.md) §Limitations.
